Gun Responsibility

After the Marjory Stoneman Douglas shooting in 2018, DeSantis said he would veto red flag laws and further gun regulations if they made it to his desk; instead choosing to sign SB 7030 into law allowing teachers to be armed in public schools. Following this he broadened the law to force local governments to pay out as much as $100,000 in damages if they are sued for enforcing gun regulations, and despite continued gun violence, he has pushed for “constitutional carry’ to be enacted in Florida, allowing residents to carry concealed guns without a permit.

Reproductive Justice

Enacted the “Reducting Fetal and Infant Mortality Act” which prohibits abortions after 15 weeks of gestation. Additionally signed SB404 requiring minors to have the written consent of parents to obtain an abortion—against the suggestions of the American Academy of Pediatricts, who signal concerns for privacy and safety of minors seeking medical care.
